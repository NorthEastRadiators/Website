We're based in Aberdeen with 40 years experience in the radiator and heat exchange services industry. North East Radiators was founded in 2023 with a passion and desire to serve any types of radiator and heat exchange repairs.

We service, clean, test, repair and recore of domestic, commercial and industrial supply radiators, oil cooler, intercoolers, fuel tankers, heat exchangers and heating batteries. We pride ourselves on exceptional, friendly and professional services with the customer always in forefront of our minds.
