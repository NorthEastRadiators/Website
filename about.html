About Us

We service, clean, test, repair and recore commercial and industrial supply radiators, oil coolers, intercoolers, fuel tankers, heat exchangers and heating batteries. We pride ourselves on exceptional, friendly and professional services with the customer always in the forefront of our minds.

Company owner, John Welsh has 40 years experience in the industry and has repaired all kinds of radiators in this time within many different industries. John likes a challenge and will take on any job no matter what condition. If he can’t fix it no one can. With all this skill, passion and knowledge, John decided to set up North East Radiators.

Currently we consist of a very small family team. However, we want to ensure our team work professionally and responsively to provide the highest quality service for our clients.

We offer a high level service which is competitive and of great value to our customers providing customer satisfaction every time.
